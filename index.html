It's a big world, and it's full of pirates...

In a sprawling, detailed chain of islands, Tilia sets out of search for her father. Unravel pirate conspiracies, discover treasures and curses from a flooded kingdom, outmaneuver powerful monsters. The world of Ocean's Heart is brimming with beautiful nature, bustling towns, and old mysteries.
