Follow their trail through the ruins of a flooded kingdom...

In a sprawling, detailed chain of islands, Tilia sets out of search for her father. Unravel pirate conspiracies, discover treasures and curses from a kingdom overtaken by the sea, outmaneuver powerful monsters. Ocean's Heart is a handcrafted single-player action-RPG, similar to classic Zelda games. The world is brimming with beautiful nature, bustling towns, and old mysteries, rendered in gorgeous, bright pixel art.



Explore

You'll explore a detailed and beautiful archipelago, brimming with secrets hidden in misty forests, rocky coves, isolated mountain peaks, saltwater marshes, and many other locations. Walk through the ruins of a flooded kingdom, or roam bustling city streets. Every area is filled with unique sidequests, mysteries, and dangers.


Battle

You'll battle fearsome abominations from the sea, pirate captains, and sometimes useless dweebs who think they can fight you for some reason. You'll find many weapons to defend yourself, and by searching for special materials, you can get blacksmiths to upgrade your weapons until you're demolishing monsters who once put up fierce fights.


Unravel

As you hunt for Tilia's father, you'll stumble across plots to overthrow cities, art heists, hidden vaults deep in mountains, ancient, crumbling fortresses, and everyday people trying to make it in the world. Choose to discover the forgotten tragedy of an ancient kingdom, shape the future of the current world, or keep your head down and focus on your own mission.



Features

  • Story driven, single-player RPG
  • 15+ hours of content
  • Sprawling, gorgeously pixeled world rich with secrets
  • Varied enemies and tense boss fights
  • Numerous weapons, spells, and abilities to find
  • Unique, character driven sidequests
  • Foraging and crafting elements
  • Quirky charm and humor
